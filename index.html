‚ö†Ô∏è Identified Issues & Recommendations

Areas Needing Clarification
1. SCENE NAMING & BREAKS (Act II Issue)
The middle section (00:15:00 - 01:00:00) has overlapping demonstrations without clear scene boundaries. Currently runs:
- Tool selection (free tier vs paid)
- Cost analysis
- GitHub/Visual Studio setup
- Cursor AI introduction
- Repository creation

RECOMMENDATION: Add chapter markers every 5-10 minutes with distinct scene names like:
"The Tool Market" | "The Free Path" | "The Professional Path" | "The Local Development Scene"
2. HOOK CLARITY
The hook (golden microphone) is powerful but abstract. The personal connection could be stronger. The war refugee example is more emotionally clear, but could come earlier to establish stakes more immediately.
3. STAGE TRANSITIONS
Some transitions between stages feel abrupt. "The Evolution of Tools" metaphor (00:10:15) could be expanded into its own scene to improve pacing.
4. PROBLEM-CLIMAX GAP
The problem is established (war refugee, enterprise resistance) but the climactic moment of solving it could be more explicit. The moment when viewers realize "I can do this too" needs emphasis.
